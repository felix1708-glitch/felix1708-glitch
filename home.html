Young Africans Sport Club

Young Africans Sports Club, commonly referred as Yanga is a Tanzanian professional football club based at Jangwani ward of llala District in Dar es Salaam Region, Tanzania. Founded in 1935, the club play their home games at the Benjamin Mkapa Stadium in Miburani ward of Temeke District.
